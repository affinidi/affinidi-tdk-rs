Key Finding
Affinidi TDK-RS is the most comprehensive modern SSI stack in Rust. With 35 crates covering DIDComm v2 (only in Rust), OID4VC (VCI + VP + SIOPv2), SD-JWT-VC, mdoc/mDL (only in Rust SSI), BBS+ ZKP, eIDAS 2.0 Trust Lists (only in Rust), and 9+ DID methods including did:webvh and did:peer — it is the only library spanning all four pillars: secure messaging, credential formats, exchange protocols, and trust infrastructure — while remaining fully platform-independent. Built in 1 year with 92k LOC, 1,009 tests, and the fastest adoption rate (25k downloads/month). Remaining gaps (PQC, security audit, WASM) are additive, not architectural.
